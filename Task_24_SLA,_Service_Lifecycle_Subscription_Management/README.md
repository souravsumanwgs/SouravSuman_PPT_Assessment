 SLA, Service Lifecycle & Subscription Management (Easy)

## 📌 Objective
Understand **Azure subscription**, **Service Level Agreement (SLA)**, and **service lifecycle basics**.

---

## 🛠️ Services Used
- Microsoft Azure Portal  
- Subscriptions  
- Help + Support  
- Service Health & Resource Health  

---

## 🌐 Implementation Steps

### Step 1: Open Subscriptions
- Search for **Subscriptions** in the Azure Portal.  
- Open your **active Subscription** (Sandbox or Student Subscription).

### Step 2: Review Subscription Overview
- Check subscription **details** like:  
  - Subscription Name  
  - Subscription ID  
  - Billing information  
  - Subscription type

### Step 3: Check SLA Documentation
- Go to **Help + Support** section.  
- Search for **Azure SLA** documentation.  
- Read SLA details for **Virtual Machines** and **Storage services**.

### Step 4: Service Health
- Open **Service Health** to check current service issues (if available).  
- Open **Resource Health** to review health of individual resources.

## 🔍 Key Learnings
- **Azure Subscriptions** define access, billing, and management scope.  
- **SLA** guarantees uptime and performance commitments for Azure services.  
- **Service Health & Resource Health** help monitor the health and availability of services.  
- Understanding subscription and SLA helps plan reliable cloud deployments.
